## Mono vs Stero

About mono and stereo audio.



### fixing a stereo imbalance

![image]()



Mix a stereo track down to mono

Unless you change the settings on the Zoom H4N Pro before you begin, the default recording will be a stereo file

- In the example above, the right microphone picked up the audio more than the left, creating an unbalanced sound (note the right channel has a largerwaveform)
- For a single voice, spoken word recording this will be distracting to your listener
- A recommended solution is to mix down the stereo track to a mono track
- This does not apply if you are recording a soundscape or ambient noise where a volume imbalance between the left and right channels may be desired to create a sense of space and movement

### transforming stereo to mono

Mix Stereo Down to Mono

- Select the stereo track byclicking the track header or theSelect button
- Click Tracks > Mix > Mix StereoDown to Mono
