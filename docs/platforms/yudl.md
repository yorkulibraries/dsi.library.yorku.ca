# York University Digital Library (YUDL)

[York University Digital Library (YUDL)](https://digital.library.yorku.ca/) is an online platform for stewarding, preserving, and providing access to unique cultural heritage materials held by the university.

YUDL operates as part of a broader ecosystem of regional, national, and international cultural heritage repositories. Its role includes long-term preservation, responsible access, and consistent description of digital objects.

---

## York University Digital Library Resources

### Metadata and Description

#### Supporting Policies

- [Policy on Creating Person, Family, or Corporate Body Vocabulary Values](https://digital.library.yorku.ca/about/metadata/policies/creating-person-family-or-corporate-body-vocabulary-values)  
  Guidelines for creating and maintaining authority records for people and organizations.

- [Policy on Creating Subject Terms](https://digital.library.yorku.ca/about/metadata/policies/creating-subject-terms)

- [Policy on Geographic Subject Names](https://digital.library.yorku.ca/about/metadata/policies/geographic-subject-names)  
  Rules for geographic naming conventions in metadata.
